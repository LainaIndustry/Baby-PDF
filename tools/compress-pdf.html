About Compress PDF Tool

Our Compress PDF tool reduces the file size of your PDF documents, making them easier to share via email or upload to the web. The compression is lossless, meaning the quality remains high.

When to use PDF compression:

  • Sending PDFs via email
  • Uploading to websites with file size limits
  • Saving storage space on your device
  • Improving loading times for web PDFs
