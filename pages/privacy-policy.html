No Data Collection Policy

BabyPDF is committed to protecting your privacy. Our tools are designed with privacy as a core principle:

1. No File Uploads

All PDF processing happens locally in your web browser. Your files never leave your device and are never uploaded to our servers.

2. No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information. No registration or login is required to use our tools.

3. Cookies

We use only essential cookies for website functionality. We do not use tracking cookies or analytics cookies.

4. Third-Party Services

We may display advertisements from Google AdSense. These services have their own privacy policies.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: privacy@babypdftools.com
